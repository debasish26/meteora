Rules For Meteora

  • Registration fee for every team is 3000/- and the last day of registration is 28th January 2024.
  • Participating teams need to report to the registration desk at 8.30 am. compulsorily with the ID cards of all the respective team participants (Presence for the inaugural is mandatory).
  • Each team will be allotted a circus-carnival element as their team name by drawing lots.
  • Repeat participation for team members is allowed only if the timings of the events they want to participate in does not clash.
  • There will be an overall champion and runners - up, determined by the cumulative score of each team at the end of every event.
  • Participants are requested to report to the respective event venue at least 15 minutes prior to its commencement. Any delay during the performance will lead to disqualification of the team.
